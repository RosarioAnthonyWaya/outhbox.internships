02 / Our perks

We believe good work deserves good conditions

We want our people to grow while doing meaningful work. Here’s what you can expect when you join the Outhbox Internships team:

Join our team

Creative Freedom

We value initiative and new ideas. You’ll have space to experiment, suggest better ways, and make things happen.

No Corporate Politics

We’re a small, focused team that values results and respect. You’ll never have to climb through office hierarchies or deal with unnecessary drama.

Career growth

You’ll learn fast, work on real systems, and get mentorship that prepares you for leadership — not just more tasks.

Hybrid Freedom

You can work from home or our Lagos space, depending on what helps you focus. Remote contributors in the UK? Also welcome

Shared Learning Culture

Everyone here is learning something new — automation, design, security, or management. We share what we learn, hold internal workshops, and sometimes just sit together to figure things out.

Work on Global Projects

We collaborate with clients across Nigeria, the UK, and beyond. You’ll gain exposure to international standards, cultures, and expectations — something few early-stage roles offer.
